About

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Basic Medical Sciences at Tsinghua University, working at the interface of cancer systems biology, bioinformatics, single-cell genomics, and spatial transcriptomics. My research focuses on understanding tumor ecosystem heterogeneity, especially in pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma, through integrative multi-omics analysis.

I am interested in building high-resolution atlases, deciphering tumor microenvironment organization, and identifying clinically relevant mechanisms that shape cancer progression and therapeutic response.
